This page is used to inform visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decided to use our Service.

**2. Information Collection and Use**
The app does not require account creation on our servers. The data you explicitly provide (subscription names, costs, payment history) is stored locally on your device within the application's database.

The app may request the following permissions:
* **Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS):** Used solely to send local reminders about upcoming payments and trial expirations.
* **Biometrics:** Used for local authentication to secure app access if enabled in Settings. We do not collect or store your biometric data; authentication is handled securely by the Android operating system.
* **Internet Access:** Required to fetch up-to-date currency exchange rates.

**3. Third-Party Services**
The app does use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you or provide functionality:
* **ExchangeRate-API:** (https://www.exchangerate-api.com/terms) Used for currency conversion features. Please refer to their privacy policy for more details.

**4. Data Storage and Security**
* **Local Storage:** Your subscription and payment data are stored locally on your device.
* **Backup & Restore:** The app allows you to export and import your data via JSON files. You are responsible for the safe storage and transfer of these backup files.
* **Security:** If you set a PIN code, it is stored securely in the device's local preferences.

**5. Data Deletion**
You can delete specific subscription or payment records at any time through the app interface. You can also perform a full data reset via the app settings ("Reset Database") or by uninstalling the application from your device.

**6. Changes to This Privacy Policy**
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

**7. Contact Us**
